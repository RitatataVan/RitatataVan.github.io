About

Hi, welcome to my page! I am a final year undergraduate student at the University of Toronto, studying statistics specialist, economics major and math minor.

My research interest broadly lies at the intersection of statistics, machine learning, finance and healthcare. I am driven by the belief that great innovation can arise from the intersection of different disciplines.

This summer I developed Acute Concussion Triage Avatar at UHN, a triage tool to be used to identify individuals in the acute and sub-acute stages post-concussion who are at high-risk for persisting symptoms and poor outcomes who should be referred to and seen in specialized concussion clinics. Before that, I was a Data Science Intern at Moneris.

Additionally, I enjoy participating in various Kaggles and Datathon events! I'm currently a Kaggle Master with 1 gold, 3 silver, and 2 bronze medals.
